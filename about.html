The Jesus Prayer 40 challenge is about introducing Eastern Catholics and others alike about the rich spiritual gift that is the Jesus Prayer.

Over the course of 40 days, 40 individuals will encounter Christ through the Jesus Prayer, weekly videos, spiritual texts, and regular Zoom calls with fellow pilgrims from around the world.

Please join consider joining the latest group on Facebook. Just search for Jesus Prayer 40!
